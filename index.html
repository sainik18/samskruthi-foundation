WHY

Cutting across all the lines of social fabric (of the nation), many people concerned with future of the nation, are discussing about the run-down of the value systems and consequential impairments in society. We in the foundation also did a number of brain storming exercises and finally zeroed down on one single point as an answer to all the questions. That is “running down of our cultural values systems and the need for arresting these trends without further loss of time” and hence the effort.

WHAT

The foundation also works like a conduit between the intellectual groups and people at gross root level, who would be working for the cause with love and devotion. At present it is identifying and aligning with people who are already working in similar fields, as we believe in humble beginnings.

WHEN

The foundation already initiated its work in a humble way, though. Now it is time for expansion of this activity by broad basing the work force as well as the think tank. In fact this is the point where the likeminded people are being gathered to take the mantle forward and far and wide.

WHERE

The foundation believes that the work to be done has no “area specifics” the reason being that the need is there everywhere. As being experienced by one and all, the invasion is from all corners and is spreading to all walks of life. It is also a fact that many people who are deeply hurt and concerned with this run down are looking for a suitable forum. And we are sure that for all such people this is the right forum.

HOW

In fact this is the real part of the concern where all the thoughts, concerns and feelings need to get transformed in to actions and as actions in proper direction... As already stated in the foregoing, the foundation has taken a view that it is always better to work with someone who is already in the field with similar aspirations, goals and concerns; rather than trying to go independently. A few of the programs could also be taken up by foundation like awareness programs.

In this area the foundation has already aligned in the following activities.

  1. Personality Development programs for the rural youth and creating awareness.
  2. Imparting information regarding our rich culture and heritage and the need to retain it.
  3. Bala samskara Kendra’s in rural areas.
  4. Conducting yoga camps (right way to live a life)
  5. Arranging lectures from the learned ones regarding the values of life and the need for role models.
